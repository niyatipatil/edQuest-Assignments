About Ladakh

Ladakh, often referred to as the "Land of High Passes," is a region in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir. Known for its stunning landscapes and unique culture, Ladakh is a popular destination for travelers seeking adventure and tranquility.

Ladakh is renowned for its monasteries, picturesque villages, and dramatic mountain scenery.

Highlights of Ladakh

  • Leh - The largest town and a gateway to the region.
  • Hemis Monastery - One of the most famous and largest monasteries in Ladakh.
  • Pangong Lake - A beautiful high-altitude lake that extends from India to Tibet.
  • Nubra Valley - Known for its sand dunes, monasteries, and scenic landscapes.
  • Khardung La - One of the highest motorable passes in the world.
